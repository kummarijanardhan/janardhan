JNTU Campus Highlights

JNTU is acknowledged for its campus and residential facilities. Some of the highlighting features of the JNTU campus are listed below.

  1. JNTU organises different types of seminars, counselling seasons, conferences for the betterment of students.
  2. Sports competitions in inter or intra college are held on the campus or out of the campus.
  3. Many motivational speakers and famous personalities are called for the seasons and programmes by the college.
  4. There are many clubs associated with institutes such as debating, dancing, singing, skating, enactment and many more options are available for students who have interests in different areas.
  5. Freshers, Convocation, Farewell, Youth festival, Educational Trips and so on are organised by the college.

Labs

College labs are most often attached to science and pre-med classes. Lab classes vary according to whether they are for a major or to fulfill general-ed requirements. Common science classes with labs include biology, chemistry, physics and astronomy.

Largest Play Ground

A Physical education center with full fledged Gymnasium, playfields for football, hockey, cricket, baseball, kho-kho and courts for basketball, volleyball and Badminton. There are physical trainers to look after. The availability and condition of equipments are also good.

ClassRooms

Well organised and furnished classrooms and digital boards are installed in every class to make sure a student must have a better learning experience.
