Campus placements are a common occurance at some of the biggest B-school and engineering in india.They provide an opportuninty for students to start their careers in professional world,immediatly after they have completed their education.Campus placements in india,which are usually held during the final year of graduation for most students,also enable them interact with the company's representatives & industry professionals during the placement drive which help them lay the foundation of their future careers even they network with potential contacts in their field


Skill Training & More Intenship Opportunities


The way to give students exposure and some skills is through the means of internships.More and more companies are on the hunt for young talent and with campus placements for internships,both students and companies can have the opportunities to work together for a specific time period and aid mutual growth for both the candidates as well as the organisation they're working as an intern.


More Companies In Campus Placements


Given the number of students that apply for top campus placements in india at college level,the colleges usually create very few opportunities for its students to get employed,while lack of skils makes up for just one reason,another important problem is the lack of appropriate number of companies that come for placements. More companies need to be invited for campus placements in india.Having more companies would ensure that students from diverse backgrounds are getting enough opportunities to understand the selection process and to also get selected.
